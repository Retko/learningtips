Basic Tips

Choose what works for you.
  • Make it interesting.
  • Plan studying time and stick to the plan.
  • Take minimum 5-10 minute breaks every hour of studying.
  • Do physical exercises while on break.
  • Get enough sleep.
  • Choose good place to study. Sometime it’s good not to study at home. Library, coffee house etc. can be much better.
  • Turn off the TV.
  • Turn off the music. Or at least listen to music without words.
  • Reconsider coffee. Can boost for a while, but then it becomes worse.
  • Learn important first, and later go for details.
  • Use highlighter.
  • Read books. Studying from bullet points is not enough.
  • Try to read more efficiently. See more in Study Reading part.
  • Explain material to someone else. This will improve your memorization.
  • Use Flashcards.
  • "Repetition is the mother of skill."
  • Use glossary in the books and understand the terms.
  • Use Wikipedia. First paragraph (or even first sentence) gives a lot of summary information.
  • Watch Youtube educational videos. There are great tutorials and even whole lessons.
  • Block Social Networking, like Facebook.
  • You can try study in groups.
  • Visualize.
  • Have job environment on your mind while studying.
  • Practice concentration. See more in concentration section, here.
  • Make use of explanatory dictionary, even if you are English native speaker.
  • Understanding is better than remembering.
  • Don’t procrastinate.
  • Study under slightly colder conditions and fresh air.
